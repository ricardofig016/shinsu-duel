Shinsu Duel: A Tower of God CCG

Shinsu Duel is a 1vs1 collectible card game (CCG) inspired by SIU's Tower of God. Players build decks, deploy units, and use abilities to destroy their opponent's lighthouses while protecting their own.

Objective

Destroy your opponent's lighthouses. Each lighthouse represents one point of HP. You lose when you have 0 lighthouses.

Resources

Shinsu

  1. Required to play all cards
  2. Start with 1 shinsu in round 1
  3. Increases by 1 per round (max 10 at round 10)
  4. After round 10: Stays at 10 per round
  5. Up to 2 unspent shinsu carry over to next round
  6. Max per round: 12 shinsu (10 + 2 carried over)
  7. Abilities may cost shinsu

Lighthouses

  1. Start with 20 lighthouses
  2. Can gain additional lighthouses
  3. Losing all lighthouses ends the game

Board

  1. Hand
  2. Deck
  3. Lighthouses
  4. Shinsu
  5. Battlefield
    1. Frontline: Fisherman, Scout, Wave Controller
    2. Backline: Spear Bearer, Light Bearer

Gameplay

Round Phases

  1. Start of Round
    1. Shinsu recharges
    2. Each player draws one card
  2. Player Turns
    1. Players alternate actions
    2. Each action is a turn
  3. Round End
    1. Both players must pass consecutively to end round

Actions

During your turn, choose one:

  1. Play a card
  2. Use an ability
  3. Pass the turn

Card Types

Unit Cards

Represent characters from Tower of God:

  1. Deployed to battlefield positions
  2. Have HP and shinsu cost
  3. Have traits and affiliations
  4. Can only target backline enemies when frontline is empty
  5. Can only target lighthouses when entire enemy board is empty
  6. Shinheuh
    1. Special units
    2. Can only be summoned by animas

Consumables

Single-use items:

  1. Provide immediate effect
  2. May target units or have global effect
  3. Playing a consumable ends your turn
  4. Examples: Lightning Pill, Ignition spells

Equipment

Attach to units for enhancements:

  1. Apply to deployed allied units
  2. Provide buffs or special abilities
  3. Cannot be removed
  4. Returns to hand when unit dies
  5. Equipping ends your turn
  6. Examples: Green April, Zahard's Ring

Landmarks

Significant Tower locations:

  1. Only 1 landmark per player on battlefield
  2. Provide battlefield-wide effects
  3. Playing new landmark destroys previous one
  4. Examples: Hidden Floor, Hell Train

Card Mechanics

Unit Stats

  1. Shinsu Cost (blue circle)
  2. HP (heart icon)
  3. Abilities (below art)
  4. Passive (top-right)

Unit Positions

  1. Fisherman: Tank damage and deal damage
  2. Light Bearer: Provide utility, buffs and lighthouses
  3. Scout: Debuff enemies and disrupt synergy
  4. Spear Bearer: Deal massive damage
  5. Wave Controller: Area damage and shinsu manipulation

Traits & Affiliations

Provide modifiers and synergies (detailed in Vocabulary)

Deck Building Rules

  1. Exactly 30 cards per deck
  2. Maximum 3 copies of same card
  3. Starting hand: 4 cards

Vocabulary

Keywords

  1. Cleanse: Remove all negative traits
  2. Destroy n: Destroy n enemy lighthouses
  3. Fast: Ability/spell doesn't end turn
  4. Silence: Remove all positive traits
  5. Spend n: Pay n shinsu to use ability

Traits

Positive Traits

  1. Barrier: Negate next damage instance
  2. Bloodthirsty: +1 HP when killing a unit
  3. Creator: Create 1 lighthouse when deployed
  4. Dealer: Draw 1 card at round start
  5. Immune: Immune to negative effects
  6. Last One Standing: +4 HP/+4 damage when only ally
  7. Lethal: Kill units when damaging them
  8. Pierce: Destroy 1 lighthouse when dealing damage
  9. Reflect: Deal 1 damage when damaged
  10. Regenerate: Full HP at round end
  11. Resilient: -1 damage from all sources
  12. Ruthless: +1 damage per lighthouse destroyed
  13. Sharpshooter: Target any enemy
  14. Strong: +1 damage
  15. Taunt: Enemies must target me if possible

Negative Traits

  1. Burned: 1 damage per turn (round duration)
  2. Cursed: 1 damage when using abilities
  3. Doomed: Die at round end
  4. Exhausted: -1 damage
  5. Ghost: Doesn't protect backline
  6. Heavy: Abilities cost +1 shinsu
  7. Poisoned: 1 damage per round end
  8. Rooted: Can't switch positions
  9. Stunned: Can't use abilities
  10. Weak: +1 damage from all sources

Trait Colors

  1. Positive:
    1. Defensive Buffs (Yellow)
    2. Damage Buffs (Orange)
    3. Utility Buffs/HP Gain (Blue)
  2. Negative:
    1. Damage Debuffs (Red)
    2. Utility Debuffs (Purple)

Future Features

Team Leader

  1. Special effect when designated as leader
  2. Only one leader per player

Evolution

  1. Units evolve under specific conditions
  2. Improves stats/abilities and changes art
  3. Example: Khun Ran evolves with Lightning Pill
